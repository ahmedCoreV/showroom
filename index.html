Agent Outreach & Showroom Qualification Console

How to run the search — step by step

0
Open the console. Everything you type is saved automatically to this chat's private storage — close it and come back anytime, your progress is kept.
1
Set up contacts (one time). In the Pipeline tab, key contacts are pre-filled where publicly listed. For the rest, click the agency's website link, find their commercial/retail leasing contact, and paste the email into the row. Where a landlord uses a leasing portal instead of email (e.g. Majid Al Futtaim's Partner Connect), the row says so.
2
Fire the outreach. Click Generate outreach on a row. A pre-written email opens with the full spec and the AED 400,000 all-in ceiling, and it references the attached brief. Click Download brief PDF in the same window, then Open in email app (or Copy), drag the PDF into the email, and send. When sent, click Mark as sent — this starts the follow-up clock.
3
Let the follow-up engine work. After 4 days with no reply, the row turns amber: Follow-up 1 due, with a ready chaser email. Five days later, Follow-up 2 due. Seven days after that it suggests a call or close-out. Daily routine: open the console, check the "Follow-ups due" number, click through the amber rows. Ten minutes.
4
When an agent replies, set their stage to Responded. A "Qualify their option" button appears on their row and jumps you to the Qualify tab with the agency pre-selected.
5
Qualify the property. Enter the unit's details from the agent's email and click Run qualification. Hard gates (over AED 400k all-in, ceiling under 3.0 m, no XP1 delivery route, no 24/7 A/C, insufficient floor loading, no W/C possible) = automatic FAIL. Soft issues (shell & core without rent-integrated fit-out, weak frontage, no valet, short lease term) = CONDITIONAL with warnings. Clean = PASS. Copy the reason lines back to the agent as feedback — it trains them on your spec fast.
6
Build the shortlist. Save each qualified option; the Shortlist tab ranks them by score across both cities. That table is your viewing schedule — highest score first.

The loop: outreach → timer → chase → reply → qualify → shortlist. The console handles memory and discipline; you handle sending and negotiating.

Stages advance automatically when you generate emails. Follow-up 1 becomes due 4 days after outreach; Follow-up 2 due 5 days after that. Add each agency's retail-leasing contact email once — it's saved for all future emails. Data is stored privately in this chat's storage.
